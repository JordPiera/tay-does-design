About Me

I'm Tay, a first-year design student with a strong passion for creativity and visual storytelling. My work is often inspired by my love for nature and landscape photography, where I capture the beauty of the world around me and translate those ideas into thoughtful, impactful design.

I pride myself on a strong work ethic, approaching every project with dedication, precision, and attention to detail. Whether it's developing a concept, refining a layout, or producing a final piece, I am committed to delivering high-quality results that are both visually engaging and purpose-driven.

For me, design is more than aesthetics, it's about crafting experiences, telling stories, and creating work that resonates. I'm eager to continue learning, refining my skills, and bringing fresh ideas to every creative challenge.
